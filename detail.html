"A journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step." _____

This proverb emphasizes that every great task or important goal starts with small steps. To achieve big dreams, one must begin with initial actions and never be afraid of starting small.

"An inch of time is an inch of gold, but an inch of gold cannot buy an inch of time." _____

This proverb highlights the preciousness of time, reminding us that time is even more valuable than gold. Nothing can replace lost time, so we must use it wisely.

"Don't Fear going slowly; fear standing still."
_____

This proverb emphasizes the importance of continuous progress. Even slow progress is better than no progress at all. What matters most is to keep moving forward.

"Water can carry a boat; it can also overturn it." _____

What gives you power can also destroy you. For example, just as people can support a ruler, they can also bring him down; or wealth can help a person, but also ruin them.
